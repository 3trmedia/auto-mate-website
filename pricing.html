FAQ

Pricing questions

How much does this cost?

Pricing is scoped after we talk through where you're losing the most revenue. We identify the biggest bottleneck and give you the math on what it's costing you before you spend a dollar.

How long does it take to build?

Most systems are built and live within 2 to 4 weeks, depending on how many pieces you need.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Service-based businesses generating $500K to $5M a year in revenue, typically with a crew, real demand, and no one making sure every lead becomes a booked job.

Do I have to sign a long contract?

No long-term contract is required to get started. Founding clients agree to be an early case study in exchange for founding rates, and details are worked out directly with Kaden before anything is built.
