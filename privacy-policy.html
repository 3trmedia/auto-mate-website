What We Collect

When you apply through this site, we collect your name and the industry you selected. We do not require or collect payment information, and we do not collect information beyond what you submit through the application form.

How We Use It

We use the information you submit to review your application and, if it's a fit, to reach out about becoming a founding client. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site, such as which pages are viewed and which buttons are clicked. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Data Storage

Application submissions are sent directly to Kaden's email. We keep this information only as long as needed to evaluate your application and, if you become a client, for the duration of our working relationship.
