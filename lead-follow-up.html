Why It Matters

Never Let a Quote Go Cold

A quote with no follow-up rarely turns into a job on its own. This piece keeps working a lead long after the first message, without anyone on your team having to remember to do it.

  • Every quote gets followed up until it's answered
  • Leads hear from you 5 to 8 times, not once
